There Are No Dumb Questions

Internal issues

How do tables, chairs and benches know that they are tables, chairs and benches? If I call the table a star and the bench a table? (10-12 years old)

Answer:

How to organize notes, etc. is appropriate? Can you not be too heavy? (12-16 years old)

Why so many sayings or idioms are contradictory, but no one mentions them? Which should I live according to? (10-13 years old)

External issues

Communication, communication

How do I know my speech is engaging and not drowsy?
How to speak to attract others?
How to speak with confidence?
As an event witness, how to have the audience thinking?
When I speak in public, what do I want to express? what can i bring 1. just one major Idea. 2. Give a reason to care.
When I am preparing for an interview, how can I tell a story so that the interviewer thinks that I am a suitable person?
When I am chatting with my friends, how can I express myself to make both parties happy?
If I had to talk, what would I say?
Does the structure of a speech matter? Is the total score always the best? Does the story have to be reversed? How to reason or express the facts can be accepted by others?
